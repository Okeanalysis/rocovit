COMMON QUESTIONS

Pricing & Bundles

How do preps work? +
Each "prep" is one complete financial preparation for one SME client. This includes bookkeeping automation, tax computation, and full workpaper generation for a specific financial period.
What if I run out of preps? +
You can purchase a new bundle at any time. Your validity period will reset to the new bundle's duration, and any remaining preps from your previous bundle will be added to your new balance.
Do preps expire? +
Yes, preps must be used within the validity period of your bundle (60, 90, or 180 days). This ensures we can maintain high-performance processing power for all active firms.
Can I switch bundles? +
Yes, you can upgrade to a larger bundle at any time to unlock lower unit prices and longer validity periods. The change takes effect immediately.
Is there a setup fee? +
No. Rocovit is a pay-as-you-go platform designed to scale with your firm. There are no setup fees, implementation costs, or hidden maintenance charges.
