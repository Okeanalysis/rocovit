THE PROBLEM

It is not a money problem. It is a proof problem.

Banks, investors, government procurement offices, and immigration authorities all require the same thing before they say yes - credible financial statements prepared by a qualified accountant. Without them, your business is invisible to the formal financial system regardless of how well it performs.

Rocovit gives accounting firms the tools to produce that proof - quickly, affordably, and across seven different compliance formats simultaneously. When your accountant uses Rocovit, your business gets every document it needs to access the formal economy.

WHAT FINANCIAL STATEMENTS UNLOCK

Five doors your financial statements open.

Every door requires the same key - credible financial statements produced by a qualified accountant.

₦
DOOR 01

Access business financing

Banks and microfinance institutions require at least two years of financial statements before approving a business loan, overdraft, or credit facility. Without them your application is rejected regardless of your revenue or repayment capacity.

WHO REQUIRES THIS: Commercial banks - Microfinance institutions - DFIs - Credit cooperatives
✓
DOOR 02

Win government and corporate tenders

The Bureau of Public Procurement requires certified financial statements for all federal contract bids. Most SMEs are disqualified at the financial documentation stage - not because they cannot deliver the work, but because their financials are missing or incorrectly formatted.

WHO REQUIRES THIS: Federal and state procurement offices - Corporate procurement teams - NGOs and international organisations
←
DOOR 03

Raise equity investment

Venture capital firms, angel investors, private equity, and development finance institutions all require structured, auditable financial statements before committing capital. Most SMEs have never produced financials at this standard.

WHO REQUIRES THIS: Venture capital firms - Angel investors - Private equity - DFIs - Impact investors
✈
DOOR 04

Apply for international visas and residency

UK Innovator Founder visas, UAE investor residency, and Canadian business immigration programmes all require certified personal and business financial statements. This is a service most accounting firms do not currently offer - and a market most SME owners do not know exists.

WHO REQUIRES THIS: UK Home Office - UAE GDRFA - IRCC Canada - Other immigration authorities
§
DOOR 05

Defend your tax position

FIRS and equivalent tax authorities can challenge your tax filing if it is not supported by compliant financial statements. Credible financials protect you from penalties, back-taxes, and audit risk. They also position you to claim legitimate deductions your accountant can only identify when the books are properly structured.

WHO REQUIRES THIS: FIRS - State internal revenue services - Tax tribunals - External auditors
