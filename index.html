THE SOLUTION

Where QuickBooks and Xero stop at bookkeeping, Rocovit starts at production.

Rocovit is a financial statement production engine. It ingests raw financial data from four sources - bank statements, open banking feeds, bookkeeping ledgers, and prior year financials - and produces complete IFRS-compliant financial statements and seven compliance output packs automatically. Built on IFRS for SMEs adopted across 70+ countries. One engine. Every market.
