Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device on return visits and remember certain information about your session. Rocovit uses cookies to make the platform work correctly and to understand how it is being used in aggregate.

2. The Cookies We Use

Two categories only:

Essential cookies - required for the platform to function. Cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

Cookie name Purpose Duration Type
rocovit_session Maintains your authenticated session so you do not need to log in on every page Session Essential
rocovit_csrf Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks Session Security
rocovit_currency Remembers your selected display currency across pages 12 months Preference
rocovit_consent Records your cookie preference 12 months Essential

Analytics cookies - help us understand how the platform is used in aggregate. No individual profiling. No data shared with advertising networks.

Cookie name Purpose Duration Type
_analytics_id Assigns an anonymous identifier to measure unique visits 24 months Analytics
_analytics_session Tracks the current session for aggregate usage reporting Session Analytics

3. Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use:

  • Advertising or targeting cookies
  • Remarketing or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third party behavioural profiling cookies
  • Any cookies that track you across websites outside of rocovit.com

4. Third Party Cookies

Rocovit does not permit third party advertising networks to set cookies on our platform. We use a limited number of third party services for infrastructure and analytics - these services process data under strict data processing agreements and do not use your data for their own advertising purposes.

5. How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Instructions for major browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Disabling essential cookies will prevent Rocovit from functioning correctly. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the platform.

6. Cookie Consent

When you first visit Rocovit we display a cookie notice that allows you to accept or decline analytics cookies. Essential cookies are always active as they are required for the platform to function. You can change your analytics cookie preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy as our use of cookies changes. We will update the date at the top of this page and notify registered users of material changes by email.

8. Contact

For questions about how we use cookies: privacy@rocovit.com.
